# CLAUDE-BRAND.md — dopl Brand & Design System

Design-only reference. For engineering facts (tech stack, revenue, terminology, DB), see `CLAUDE.md`.

---

## Wordmark

- `dopl` is **always lowercase**. Never capitalized, even at the start of a sentence.

## Colors

- `--bg-deep`: `#0D261F` (deep green, primary background)
- `--bg-sage`: `#2D4A3E` (sage, secondary surface)
- `--accent-lime`: `#C5D634` (lime, CTAs and highlights)
- `--bg-cream`: `#F3EFE8` (cream, light surfaces)

## Typography

- **Headings:** Fraunces (serif)
- **Body:** Inter (sans)
- **Numbers / tabular data:** JetBrains Mono

## Aesthetic direction

- Dark finance meets glassmorphism
- Dark theme by default (bg: `#0D261F`)
- Glassmorphism cards: `backdrop-blur` + semi-transparent surfaces
- Lime `#C5D634` reserved for CTAs
- Framer Motion for entrance animations and micro-interactions
- Screenshot-worthy UI — fund managers should want to share the interface on social
